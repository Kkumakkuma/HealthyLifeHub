## Understanding Hormonal Imbalance
Hormonal imbalance in women over 40 can manifest through a variety of symptoms. These include hot flashes, mood swings, weight gain, and fatigue. Understanding why this happens is crucial for effective management.

### Common Hormonal Changes After 40
As we age, our estrogen and progesterone levels naturally decline. This can lead to:

- **Menopause Symptoms**: Hot flashes and night sweats.
- **Mood Disorders**: Increased anxiety and irritability.
- **Weight Gain**: Particularly around the abdomen.
- **Sleep Issues**: Difficulty in achieving restorative sleep.

### The Importance of Balancing Hormones
Balancing hormones can significantly improve quality of life. Studies suggest that around 60% of women experience notable improvements in mood, sleep, and energy when utilizing the correct supplements.
