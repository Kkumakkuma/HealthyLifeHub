## Understanding What Anti-Aging Supplements Can Do

While aging is a natural part of life, many of us want to remain youthful for as long as possible. Anti-aging supplements can help in a variety of ways:

### Collagen and Elastin Support

Collagen and elastin are proteins essential for skin elasticity and texture. According to a report published by the National Institutes of Health (NIH), supplementation with collagen peptides can significantly enhance skin hydration and elasticity over time.

### Antioxidant Power

Antioxidants like Vitamins C and E, as well as Coenzyme Q10, fight against free radicals, the harmful by-products of metabolism and environmental stressors. A 2024 study in the Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology found that participants who took antioxidants for three months reported visibly smoother skin.

### Hormonal Balance

Hormones dramatically affect how our skin ages. Supplements that support hormonal balance, like Black Cohosh or Dong Quai, can help maintain youthful skin. The Mayo Clinic recommends these for women undergoing menopause, noting the importance of regulating skin hydration.
