## Overview of Immune Health Supplements

Immune health supplements come in various forms, typically focusing on enhancing your body’s natural response to illness. The most common ingredients include:

### Vitamins

- **Vitamin C**: Known for its antioxidant properties and role in boosting the immune system. The research consistently shows that adults should aim for at least 65 to 90 mg daily (NIH).
- **Vitamin D**: As I discovered, 1,000 to 2,000 IU a day can significantly enhance immune performance according to the Vitamin D Council.

### Minerals

- **Zinc**: Essential for immune system development and function, with optimal intake ranging from 8 mg (women) to 11 mg (men).
- **Selenium**: Research published in the *Journal of Global Health* suggests a daily intake of about 55 mcg can improve immune response.

### Herbal Ingredients

- **Elderberry**: Said to shorten the duration of cold and flu symptoms; however, studies are still ongoing.
- **Echinacea**: Extensive research shows that regular use may reduce the likelihood of catching colds.
