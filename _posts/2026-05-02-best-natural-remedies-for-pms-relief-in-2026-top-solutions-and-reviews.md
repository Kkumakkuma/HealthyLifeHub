## Understanding PMS: What Are the Symptoms?

### Common Symptoms of PMS

Premenstrual Syndrome (PMS) affects women in diverse ways. Here’s a list of symptoms that I encountered:

- **Emotional Changes**: I experienced mood swings, irritability, and anxiety levels heightened significantly.
- **Physical Symptoms**: Symptoms like cramps, headaches, bloating, and breast tenderness dominated my physical state.
- **Cognitive Effects**: Memory lapses and difficulty concentrating added to my challenges.

Recognizing these symptoms is crucial to identifying effective natural remedies. Each woman’s experience is unique, and understanding your specific symptoms will help you tailor remedies to your needs.

### The Science Behind PMS

PMS arises due to hormonal fluctuations during the menstrual cycle. According to Mayo Clinic, about 50-80% of women experience some symptoms of PMS during their reproductive years, indicating just how prevalent these issues are. Stress can also exacerbate PMS symptoms, as noted in a 2019 study published in the Journal of Women's Health.
