## What Most Guides Get Wrong

1. **Emphasizing Brand Over Function:** Many articles focus too much on brand names rather than evaluating the real user experience. I’ve tested solid and budget-friendly brands that often outperform well-known names.

2. **Weight Range Neglect:** A good adjustable dumbbell offers a wide weight range. I often found guides underselling models with fewer weight options that can still cater to a variety of users.

3. **Ignoring Build Quality:** Some guides don't mention how quickly some cheaper adjustable dumbbells break down. I’ve seen models under $100 lose functionality after a few months of regular use—a warning worth noting.
