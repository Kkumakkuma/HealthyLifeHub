## How Does Breathwork Work?

Breathwork involves deliberate breathing techniques designed to improve mental, emotional, and physical well-being. These practices can vary widely, from simple diaphragmatic techniques to more complex styles that include visualization and sound. 

Specifically, breathwork aims to:
- **Calm the Nervous System**: Various breathing techniques help to engage the parasympathetic nervous system, promoting relaxation.
- **Increase Oxygen Flow**: Deep breathing enhances oxygen delivery to the body's tissues, improving energy levels and reducing stress.
- **Promote Mindfulness**: Focusing on breath encourages individuals to tune into their present experience, minimizing racing thoughts.
