## What is Aromatherapy?

Aromatherapy is a holistic healing treatment that uses natural plant extracts, often in the form of essential oils, to promote physical and emotional well-being. My main goal was to manage stress and improve my overall mood during January’s cold and somewhat dreary days.

### How Aromatherapy Works

Essential oils interact with our nervous system and can have various effects depending on the oil used. Here's a quick overview:
- **Inhalation:** One of the most common methods, where oils are inhaled to send signals directly to the brain, thus influencing emotions.
- **Topical Application:** Oils can be diluted and applied to the skin, allowing absorption through hair follicles and skin to bring about physical benefits.
- **Diffusion:** Using a diffuser disperses essential oils in the air, creating a calming atmosphere.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How much should I budget for essential oils?

A: Generally, you can expect to spend between $15-$30 for quality 15 mL bottles from brands like doTERRA or Young Living.

### Q: Can I use essential oils without a diffuser?

A: Absolutely! You can apply diluted oils topically or inhale directly from the bottle for immediate effects.

### Q: How long does the aroma of essential oils typically last?

A: This varies; an essential oil diffused in a room may last anywhere from a few hours to a full day, depending on factors like oil concentration and room size.

### Q: Are there cheaper alternatives to high-end brands?

A: Yes! Brands like Plant Therapy and Eden's Garden offer quality oils at lower price points, making them excellent alternatives for those mindful of their budget.
