## How Do Weighted Blankets Work?

Weighted blankets use deep pressure touch stimulation to simulate a sensation similar to being hugged, which can decrease cortisol levels and increase serotonin. The science behind this technique is supported by research published in the journal Sleep Medicine Reviews, which indicates that deep pressure therapy helps alleviate anxiety symptoms.

As you snuggle under a weighted blanket, you can expect to experience:

- Reduced heart rate, leading to increased feelings of calm.
- Enhanced serotonin levels, which contribute to improved mood.
- Better sleep regulation due to reduced anxiety.

This kind of blanket can range from 5% to 10% of your body weight. It's essential to choose one that fits you; for example, if you weigh 150 pounds, a blanket between 12 to 15 pounds would be ideal.

## How to Choose the Right Weighted Blanket for You

As I navigated through various brands and types of weighted blankets, I discovered specific criteria that could significantly influence your decision. Here’s a comprehensive guide based on my experiences.

### Weight Considerations

Most people recommend a blanket that’s 10% of your body weight, but it may vary based on personal preferences and comfort levels. Personally, I began with a 15-pound Gravity Blanket, but after feeling the benefits, I tried a 20-pound Luna Weighted Blanket, which provided a more comforting enveloping feel that suited my anxiety better.

### Materials

The fabric of the weighted blanket plays a crucial role. For warmer months, consider breathable materials like bamboo or linen. In cold seasons, heavier materials like minky or fleece can add comfort. I realized that the Baloo Living blanket's cotton weave was perfect for winter use, while my YnM blanket excelled during summer, thanks to its cooling fabric.

### Size & Design

A blanket that fits your bed size can enhance your experience. I made a misstep when I purchased a twin-size blanket for my queen bed, which meant my partner frequently ended up without covers. Make sure to select a size that will adequately cover both yourself and the mattress.

### Maintenance & Durability

Durability matters when it comes to materials and washing care. Some weighted blankets can be machine washed, while others require specific care. After washing my Tranquillo multiple times, I was thrilled to see it held its shape and softness, emphasizing the importance of checking maintenance instructions.

### Brand Reputation

Brand reputation isn't just for marketing. Choices like Gravity and Bearaby come highly recommended and have established a loyal customer base due to their quality and performance. Join social media groups or forums to hear firsthand accounts and recommendations from those who have already made the purchase.
