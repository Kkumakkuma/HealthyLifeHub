## Understanding Prebiotics: What Are They and Why Do They Matter?

Before discussing costs, it’s essential to grasp what prebiotics are. Prebiotics are types of dietary fiber that feed your good gut bacteria, promoting a balanced microbiome. They can be derived from various food sources and supplements.

### Types of Prebiotics

1. **Inulin**: Found in onions, garlic, and asparagus, inulin promotes beneficial gut bacteria.
2. **Fructooligosaccharides (FOS)**: These are found in several fruits and vegetables and help stimulate beneficial bacteria growth.
3. **Galactooligosaccharides (GOS)**: Common in legumes and dairy products, GOS supports healthy gut flora.

### Health Benefits of Prebiotics

- **Improves digestion**: I noticed that my bloating incidents decreased significantly.
- **Boosts immunity**: A well-functioning gut can enhance your immune response.
- **Enhances mineral absorption**: Prebiotics can help absorb calcium and magnesium, leading to overall better bone health.
