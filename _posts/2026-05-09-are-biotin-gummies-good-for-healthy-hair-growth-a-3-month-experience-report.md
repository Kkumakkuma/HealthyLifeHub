## How Do Biotin Gummies Work?

Biotin, known as vitamin B7, plays a crucial role in converting food into energy and is key for the synthesis of keratin, a protein that forms the structure of hair, skin, and nails. Biotin gummies are appealing due to their convenient and tasty forms, better than swallowing pills.

1. **Works at a Cellular Level**: Biotin supports the production of fatty acids, which in turn strengthens hair follicles.
2. **Improves Keratin Infrastructure**: Adequate biotin levels can enhance keratin production, resulting in healthier, shinier hair.
3. **Supports Scalp Health**: It may also improve blood circulation in the scalp, promoting better nutrient absorption in hair follicles.
